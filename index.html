Cube Game Rules

How to Play:

We have N cubes on the table (You can choose the number at the form) and you can catch from the table 1 or 2 or K cubes (you choose the value of K at the form. Otherwise is 4)

To finish your round, you have to select a cube from the table and click the "Catch" button.

How to Win:

To win the game you have to catch the last cubes from the table
